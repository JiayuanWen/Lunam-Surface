* Add the string "LSMap" to first line of your map. Lunam Surface will report Map not support if this string is not present.

* Add an empty line under "LSMap" before writing your map. Any characters under "LSMap" will be skipped. 

* Use 'X' for walls. Use '~' for paths. 

* Add '^' somewhere in your map, this will be the starting point.

* Add '!' somewhere in your map, this will be the end point.

* Due to default terminal size, it is adviced to keep your map height below 19 units and length below 73 units. 

* No empty lines after your map.

* After finishing your map, place it in the "maps" folder.
